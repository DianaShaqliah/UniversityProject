Al-Azhar University

Al-Azhar University is an independent non-profit educational institution that aims to meet the needs of the Palestinian and Arab human communities of qualified human resources in various disciplines, applied scientific research and sustainable development, focusing on the employment of information and communication technologies, maintaining the authenticity of the Arab-Islamic heritage and adhering to the principles of human rights. Including justice, equality, adherence to the rule of law, transparency, tolerance, respect, non-discrimination, diversity and stakeholder partnership.
